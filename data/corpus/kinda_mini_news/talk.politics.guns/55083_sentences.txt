satire \'sa-tir\ n [MF or L; MF, fr.
L _satura_, _satira_, fr.
(lanx) satura full plate, medley, fr.
of _satur_ sated; akin to L _satis_ enough - more at SAD](1509) 1: a literary work holding up human vices and follies to ridicule or scorn.
2: trenchent wit, irony, or sarcasm used to expose and discredit vice or folly.
